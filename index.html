Bio

I am a PhD candidate in the Department of Mechanical Engineering at the Michigan State University, where I am advised by Professor Hamidreza Modares. My research interests are focused in the field of reinforcement learning for feedback control, safe control design and resilient control of cyber-physical systems.

Prior to joining Michigan State University, I received my M.Tech. degree from the Indian Institute of Technology, Kanpur in 2016, and my B.Tech. degree from the Aligarh Muslim University, Aligarh in 2013, both in Electrical Engineering.
